About Us

Your gateway to business excellence and community growth in Kananga. Join us in fostering a thriving business environment and connecting with local enterprises. The Kananga Chamber of Commerce supports businesses and promotes economic growth in Kananga, Leyte. We work closely with entrepreneurs, investors, and the local government to create opportunities, strengthen partnerships, and build a sustainable, thriving community.

Vision

To be the leading advocate for business excellence and community prosperity in Kananga, fostering a vibrant and sustainable economic environment.

Mission

To empower local businesses through advocacy, networking, and resources, while promoting economic growth and community development in Kananga.
